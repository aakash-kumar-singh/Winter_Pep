Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It is one of the most popular sports in the world.

Hockey

Hockey is a fast-paced sport played on a field or ice rink. Teams use sticks to hit a ball or puck into the opponent's goal.

Football

Football, also known as soccer, is a globally loved sport where teams aim to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing net.
